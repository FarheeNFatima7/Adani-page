|About Adani Soler

We are India's 1st and largest vertically integrated Solar PV cell and modules manufacturer with 4 GW manufacturing capacity, that offers services across the spectrum of photovoltaics manufacturing. The company offers and produces high efficiency Monofacial and Bifacial modules (in PERC Technology) with superior efficiency, higher performance and enhanced reliability. Currently, we are building the World's 1st ever Fully Integrated and Comprehensive Ecosystem of 10 GW Solar PV Manufacturing in India.
